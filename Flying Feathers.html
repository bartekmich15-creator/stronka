FLYING FEATHERS

The Great Egg Heist

The peaceful island was shattered when the greedy Green Pigs launched a sneak attack and stole the precious eggs! Now, the flock has only one mission: absolute and utter revenge. The survival of their species is at stake.

Load up the slingshot, aim carefully, and launch your furious feathered friends through the air. You must infiltrate the heavily fortified pig castles, smash through their defenses, and leave nothing but rubble behind. They started this war, but you are going to finish it.

Master the Physics

Every level is a complex puzzle governed by real-world physics and structural integrity. The pigs have built their fortresses out of wood, glass, and stone. You cannot just shoot blindly; you need to understand the materials.

Glass shatters easily, wood splinters under pressure, but stone requires a heavy hitter. Calculate your trajectory, find the structural weak points, and trigger massive chain reactions. A perfectly placed shot can bring an entire tower crashing down onto the unsuspecting pigs below!
